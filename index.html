Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 25, 2025

The BubbleMap Intel Bot does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users. All data processed by the bot is retrieved from Mobula and Bubblemaps API and is used solely for the purpose of generating token analytics and bubblemap visualizations.

This privacy-focused approach ensures your interactions with the bot remain anonymous and secure.
